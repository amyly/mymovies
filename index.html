All The Movies I've Seen
Some time in 2004, I began a list of all the movies I've ever seen. The list lives on IMDB.com, an online movie database. The site allows account holders to rate individual movies on a scale of 1-10, and have that rating be counted towards a user-aggregated average. This visualization is an exploration of all the movies I've ever seen and rated, 579 in all. Each film is represented by a box, the color of which is determined by its rating. This is also my first attempt at creating something in SVG and JavaScript, in particular the Raphaël and jQuery libraries. Enjoy! Please move your mouse and click around!
Data: IMDB.com
Tools: Raphaël, jQuery, ColorBrewer
